About VRFS

VRFS (Virtual Real Football Simulator) is an immersive multiplayer football game that blends realism, creativity, and competition. Built by and for players, it gives users full control over their gameplay, teams, and community experiences.

Players take to the pitch with precise movement, teamwork, and strategy — every pass, tackle, and shot controlled in real time. Whether you’re a club owner, a tactical coach, or a star striker, VRFS delivers a true-to-life football experience in a virtual world.

The Virtual Premier Association (VPA) is one of the top leagues within VRFS, featuring some of the best-managed clubs, talented kit designers, and over 6,800 active players. The league emphasizes fair play, creativity, and collaboration — the essence of what VRFS stands for.

Matches and tournaments are streamed live on Sportwave, the official broadcast platform for VRFS leagues, where fans can catch every highlight, transfer update, and knockout game.

Discord Rules

Respect Everyone

Respect everyone and everything whilst in the Discord. This includes any toxicity such as harassment, bullying, or abuse towards another person.

Use Appropriate Language

Do not use hateful or hurtful language or any derogatory slurs. Everyone deserves respect regardless of background or identity.

Be Mindful

Be considerate in chat; avoid offensive topics such as religion, gender, or race that could upset others.

Tolerance

Members should remain tolerant and respectful of differing perspectives and opinions.

Reporting

If you encounter issues or conflicts, open a support ticket to alert moderators.

Advertisement

No advertising of any kind — links, servers, or promotions — regardless of role or rank.

Breaking these rules may result in a strike or disciplinary action.
