Discord Rules

Respect Everyone: Respect everyone in the community. Any form of harassment, bullying, or abuse is not tolerated. We want this to be a safe space for all players.

Use Appropriate Language: Avoid hateful or offensive language. Slurs or derogatory terms are not allowed regardless of background or intent.

Be Mindful: Conversations around sensitive topics such as religion, gender, or race should be handled with care or avoided.

Tolerance: Everyone should be respectful of different perspectives and opinions.

Reporting: If you encounter a problem, please report it by opening a ticket to alert moderators.

Advertisement: No advertising or external links are allowed without staff approval.

If anyone breaks these rules, they will receive a strike and an appropriate punishment.

Make sure to also follow Discord’s official Guidelines and Terms of Service.

About VRFS

VRFS (Virtual Real Football Simulator) is the immersive virtual football game behind the VPA. Players manage clubs, control avatars, and compete in realistic leagues where strategy and teamwork matter.

VPA is one of VRFS’s flagship leagues, showcasing top talent, creative kits, and over 6,800 active players. Every match combines skill, creativity, and competition in a shared football universe.

Join the league, participate in matches, or just watch the action — VRFS brings football to life in a virtual community that thrives on passion and fair play.
